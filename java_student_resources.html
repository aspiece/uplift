Intro to Java: Student Resource Hub

Follow these steps to get started in CodeHS, complete your first assignment (MadLibs), and submit your work to Accellerate.

🚀Start Here: CodeHS + MadLibs

Use this checklist in order, especially if you are on a Chromebook and cannot use Eclipse.

  1. Create your free CodeHS account.
  2. Watch the embedded videos below.
  3. Open the CodeHS Sandbox and make sure Java is selected.
  4. Open your first assignment starter file: MadLibs.java.
  5. Use the embedded MadLibs walkthrough for extra support as needed.
  6. Submit your finished MadLibs work to Accellerate when you are done.
